ACHIEVEMENT OF ABDUL LATIFF MOHIDIN


From 1963 to 1969, Latiff was very active and diligent in creating, encompassing three different fields of art in his speech. In its oil painting, its sculpture and its printed scratches there is a remarkable alignment to the channels of different tools or materials. Through his series of creations, "Pago-Pago", "Mindscape" and "Langkawi", made Latiff an important artist in this country.



In addition to creating poetry and painting activities, Latiff used to be the unofficial leader of a group of talented young people, the group "Anak Alam" in the middle of the mother city Kuala Lumpur. After getting married, Latiff was awarded as a Creative Fellow and Guest Artist, respectively at Universiti Sains Malaysia, Penang (1977 - 1979) and Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia, Bangi (1980).



Latiff has won various major literary prizes, especially the Literary Prize in Malaysia since 1971. He won the Putera 1 Poetry Award organized by Bank Bumiputera and GAPENA. In 1984, Latiff was elected to represent Malaysia by delightly received the SEA Writer Awards in Bangkok. Beginning in 1986, Dewan Bahasa dan Pustaka has honored him as a guest writer and to fill the project as a guest writer, Latiff has devoted his energy to rewriting his experience and art creation process.
